Iskender Kebap

Check the iskender kebap recipe below

Although you use the items given, you will not get the same taste as you have in a real kebap saloon.

Yet it is worth trying. You will need:

  • Yogurt
  • Butter
  • Meat Döner
  • Pide
  • Tomato sauce

Okey. Since you have the necessary items (Döner obviously), follow the steps:

  1. Cook the döner
  2. Place the pide on a plate
  3. Place the döner on top of pide
  4. Pour Tomato sauce on the döner
  5. Finally, melt the butter and pour on the döner.
